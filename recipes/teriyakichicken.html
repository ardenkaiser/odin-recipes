Easy Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

A quick and easy lunch or dinner! This teriyaki chicken is perfect for meal planning and goes great with fried rice and broccoli.

  • teriyaki sauce
  • chicken breast or tenders
  • salt and pepper
  1. Cut thawed chicken in 2 inch cubes
  2. Add chicken to teriyaki sauce, salt, and pepper and marinate for 3 hours
  3. Set air fryer to 350 degrees and cook chicken for 5 minutes on each side
